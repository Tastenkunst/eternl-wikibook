# Staking rewards & option for auto withdrawal

Staking rewards are automatically added to your delegated stake, so there's no need to manually withdraw them unless you're sending them elsewhere or want them to contribute to your catalyst voting power. On the other hand they are not spendable as long as they’re not withdrawn. 

We've introduced an "Auto Withdrawal" feature that automatically handles the withdrawal of staking rewards when you initiate a transaction. This is designed to save on transaction fees and simplify wallet usage. 

> Wallet & Account Settings > Auto Withdrawal
{.is-info}

Transactions with this feature enabled will display a "Withdrawal" badge.

> If you opt not to use the Auto Withdrawal feature, you can still manually withdraw your staking rewards using the "Withdrawal" button on the Summary page, creating a separate transaction for this purpose.
{.is-info}
