# What Is a Recovery Phrase and Why Is It So Important?

Your 24-word recovery phrase ([seed phrase](https://github.com/Tastenkunst/eternl-wikibook/blob/main/content/faq-and-troubleshooting/security-and-privacy/what-is-a-seed-phrase.md)) is the **master key** to your entire wallet. Its purpose is to restore access to your funds on a new device if yours is lost, stolen, or broken. Anyone who has this phrase has **full control** of your funds.

::: warning **CRITICAL SECURITY RULES:** **NEVER** share it, not even with support staff. **NEVER** type it into a website or any application other than the official Eternl wallet. **DO NOT** store it digitally (no screenshots, no text files, no cloud storage). :::

::: success Instead, **write your phrase on paper** and keep it in a secure, private location. For maximum protection, consider a **metal seed storage** solution. These are fireproof, waterproof, and corrosion-resistant plates (usually steel or titanium) where you can engrave, stamp or arrange your phrase to record it permanently and safely from physical damage. :::

If you lose your recovery phrase AND you lose access to your device, your funds will be **lost forever**.

**For Enhanced Security: Hardware Wallets** For the highest level of security, consider using a hardware wallet. This device keeps your keys offline at all times. Eternl fully supports popular options like **Ledger**, **Trezor**, and **Keystone**.

**Crucially:** Even with a hardware wallet, it is still paramount to write down its recovery phrase and keep it offline and secure. The phrase remains the ultimate backup for your funds.
