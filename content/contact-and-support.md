# Contact & support

If you need help, have questions, or want to get in touch with us, you can reach us through the following channels:

| Platform     |                                          Contact                                           |
|:-------------|:------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------:|
| **Discord**  |   Join our [Eternl Discord Server](https://discord.gg/) (with a support ticket system).    |
| **Telegram** |                     Join our [Eternl Telegram Channel](https://t.me/).                     |
| **GitHub**   | If you have a feature request, please use our [GitHub Issue Tracker](https://github.com/). |
| **E-Mail**   |               Write us an email at [team@eternl.io](mailto:team@eternl.io).                |

---

::: tip
Discord is the best place to get fast support since we use a dedicated ticket system.
:::
